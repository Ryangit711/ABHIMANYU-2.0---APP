# PEOPLE MAPS BY COMPANY — 4-Target Connect Strategy

## How To Use

1. **Open LinkedIn** → search the exact search string below
2. **Find the person** who matches the title/team description
3. **Send the exact connect message** I've written
4. **Come back** and say: `Connected with [Name] at [Company]`
5. I log it, track it, ping you when next touch is due

Search strings work. Try: `"Strategy & Operations" Clio Vancouver` → LinkedIn shows you people.
